# E-Commerce-Sales-Dashboard

![E-Commerce Sales Dashbord](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bc217aae-7b65-4934-9990-f78285690efd)

This project is an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer trends, and product demand across multiple countries. The dashboard provides Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons and highlights key business KPIs for data-driven decision-making.

## Objective

> Track overall sales performance and growth.

> Compare current year metrics with Previous Year (PY) performance.

> Identify top-performing countries and products.

> Monitor customer growth and pricing trends over time.

## Tools & Technologies

Power BI

Power Query

DAX

Excel

PowerPoint (Wireframing)

## Data Preparation

Imported raw datasets into Power BI.

Cleaned and transformed data in Power Query:

Removed duplicates and null values

Standardized data types

Created fact and dimension tables

Built relationships between tables to support accurate analysis.

## DAX Measures

Total Sales: $105M+

## Dashboard Design & Visuals

Wireframed the dashboard layout in PowerPoint before implementation in Power BI.

Visuals Included:

Shape Map: Sales distribution by country

Stacked Column Chart: Monthly total sales

Line Charts: Monthly trends for sales, quantity, and average unit price

Area Chart: Customer growth over time

Donut Chart: Sales by customer segment

Stacked Bar Charts:

Top 5 products by quantity sold

Top 5 countries by quantity sold

All visuals are interactive and filter-responsive.

## Key Insights

Generated $105M+ in total sales from 9,191 customers.

Sold over 6M units with an average unit price of $17.6.

Clear visibility into YoY growth trends across sales, quantity, and customers.

Identified top-performing products and countries driving the majority of sales.
